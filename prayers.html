Stations of the Cross
✝️ Stations of the Cross

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Lord Jesus Christ, you walked this path out of love for us. I am sorry for my sins and I want to follow you with a faithful heart. Help me remain with you always.

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider how Jesus was unjustly condemned despite His innocence.

R: My Jesus, it was my sins that condemned You. I repent with all my heart. Help me to love You always. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider how Jesus carried the Cross with love for us.

R: Give me strength to carry my crosses with patience and trust in You. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Jesus falling under the weight of the Cross.

R: Help me rise again whenever I fall into sin. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider the sorrowful meeting between Jesus and His Mother.

R: Grant me love and devotion to Mary. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider how Simon helped Jesus carry His Cross.

R: Help me accept and help others in their burdens. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Veronica’s act of compassion.

R: Help me to serve You in others with love. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Jesus falling again in weakness.

R: Strengthen me to persevere in difficult times. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Jesus comforting the women of Jerusalem.

R: Grant me true repentance and compassion. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Jesus falling again, exhausted.

R: Give me strength in my weaknesses. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Jesus stripped of His garments.

R: Detach me from worldly attachments. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Jesus nailed to the Cross.

R: Bind my heart to You forever. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Jesus dying for our salvation.

R: I love You above all things. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Jesus in the arms of His Mother.

R: Let me share in Your sorrow and love. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)

V: We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You.
R: Because by Your holy Cross You have redeemed the world.

V: Consider Jesus laid in the tomb.

R: May I rise with You to eternal life. I love You, Jesus, and I repent of my sins. Never permit me to be separated from You again.

(Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be.)


Closing Prayer
Lord Jesus Christ, by Your Passion and death, grant us grace, forgiveness, and eternal life. Amen.
