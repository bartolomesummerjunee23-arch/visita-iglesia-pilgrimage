✝️ Stations of the Cross

In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

My Lord Jesus Christ, Thou hast made this journey to die for me with love unutterable; and I have so many times ungratefully abandoned Thee. But now I love Thee with my whole heart; and because I love Thee, I am sincerely sorry for ever having offended Thee. Pardon me, my God, and permit me to accompany Thee on this journey. Thou goest to die for love of me; I wish also, my beloved Redeemer, to die for love of Thee. My Jesus, I will live and die always united to Thee.

We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Consider how Jesus Christ, after being scourged and crowned with thorns, was unjustly condemned by Pilate to die on the Cross.

My adorable Jesus, it was not Pilate; no, it was my sins that condemned Thee to die. I beseech Thee, by the merits of this sorrowful journey, to assist my soul in its journey towards eternity. I love Thee, beloved Jesus; I love Thee more than myself. I repent with my whole heart of having offended Thee. Never permit me to separate myself from Thee again. Grant that I may love Thee always; and then do with me what Thou wilt.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Consider how Jesus, in making this journey with the Cross on His shoulders, thought of us and offered for us to His Father the death He was about to suffer.

My most beloved Jesus, I embrace all the sufferings Thou hast destined for me until death. I beg Thee, by the merits of the pain Thou didst suffer in carrying Thy Cross, to give me the help necessary to carry mine with perfect patience and resignation. I love Thee, Jesus, my love; I repent of ever having offended Thee. Never permit me to separate myself from Thee again. Grant that I may love Thee always; and then do with me what Thou wilt.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Consider this first fall of Jesus under His Cross. His flesh was torn by the scourges, His head crowned with thorns, and He had lost a great quantity of blood. He was so weakened that He could scarcely walk, and yet He had to carry this great load upon His shoulders.

My beloved Jesus, it is not the weight of the Cross but of my sins which has made Thee suffer so much. By the merits of this first fall, deliver me from the misfortune of falling into mortal sin. I love Thee, O my Jesus, with my whole heart; I repent of having offended Thee. Never permit me to separate myself from Thee again. Grant that I may love Thee always; and then do with me what Thou wilt.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Consider how the Son met His Mother on His way to Calvary. Jesus and Mary looked at each other, and their looks became so many arrows to wound those hearts which loved each other so tenderly.

My most loving Jesus, by the pain Thou didst suffer in this meeting, grant me the grace to be truly devoted to Thy most holy Mother. And thou, my Queen, who wast overwhelmed with sorrow, obtain for me by thy intercession a continual and tender remembrance of the Passion of thy Son. I love Thee, Jesus, my love; I repent of ever having offended Thee. Never permit me to separate myself from Thee again. Grant that I may love Thee always; and then do with me what Thou wilt.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Consider how the Jews, seeing that Jesus was nearly dead, compelled Simon of Cyrene to help Him carry His Cross.

My most sweet Jesus, I will not refuse the Cross as Simon did: I accept it, I embrace it, I accept in particular the death Thou hast destined for me, with all the pains which may accompany it. I unite it to Thy death and offer it to Thee. Thou hast died for love of me; I will die for love of Thee. Help me by Thy grace.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Consider how Veronica wiped the face of Jesus.

My most beloved Jesus, Thy face was beautiful before, but in this journey it has lost all beauty. Restore my soul by Thy grace.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Consider the second fall of Jesus.

Give me strength to persevere in grace.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Weep not for Me, but for yourselves.

Grant me repentance.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Consider the third fall.

Strengthen me in weakness.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Jesus is stripped.

Detach me from earthly things.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Jesus is nailed.

Bind my heart to Thee.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Jesus dies on the Cross.

I love Thee above all.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Jesus is taken down.

Share in His sorrow.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.
We adore Thee, O Christ, and we bless Thee.
Because by Thy holy Cross Thou hast redeemed the world.

Jesus is laid in the tomb.

Keep me united to Thee forever.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory be to the Father.

Closing Prayer
We beseech Thee, O Lord Jesus Christ, that by Thy most holy Passion and death, Thou wouldst grant us Thy grace, that it may be to us a perpetual remedy against all sin, the increase of grace, and the attainment of everlasting glory.

Grant, we beseech Thee, O Lord, that we, Thy servants, may enjoy perpetual health of mind and body; and by the glorious intercession of the Blessed Virgin Mary, ever Virgin, may be delivered from present sorrow and attain to eternal joy.

Through Christ our Lord. Amen.
